About Us

Loan App was created by House Hufflepuff. Loan App is free to use and will always be free to use. Users will have to input their initial principle, interest rate, and length of the loan to see results. The basic concept behind our formula is to calculate monthly payments based on the total principle plus total interest and divide that by the length of the loan. We also provide the total amount of interest so users can see exactly how much they are spending over principle. If a user wants to save money, they can reduce the length of the loan and check again to see how much less interest they’d be paying. Keep in mind, that means the user will have to pay an increased amount each month. To spend less overall, they’d have to pay more each month.
